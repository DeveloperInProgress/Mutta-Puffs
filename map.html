Mutta Puffs
Mutta Puffs is a sports league scheduler that automates the scheduling process parallely on multiple computers in Golem network. In the input boxes above, provide the names, abbreviations and home stadium locations of the teams participating in the league. Round-Robin leagues require even number of in order to play every team in each round so, Mutta Puffs expects even number of teams in your list. And there has to be atleast 4 teams inorder to justify the need for automation of the scheduling process.
